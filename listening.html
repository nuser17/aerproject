Listening section of IELTS

Certainly, here are some IELTS listening strategies

Overall , We have eight main strategies in IELTS listening section to achieve high scores for example 9.0 or 8.5 first one is previewing the questions and focusing on keywords,being aware of question types , anticipate answers , note-taking , practicing multitasking and checking spelling .
  • Preview the Questions: Quickly go through the questions before the recording starts to get an idea of what to listen for.
  • Focus on Keywords: Listen for keywords or key phrases in both questions and answers. These will help you identify the relevant information.
  • Be Aware of Question Types: Understand the different question types, such as multiple-choice, matching, and completion, to tailor your approach accordingly.
  • Anticipate Answers: Try to predict what kind of information you might hear based on the question, but stay flexible in case the recording provides unexpected details.
  • Note-Taking: Use the provided time to read and write notes on the question paper. This can help you organize information and remember key points.
  • Practice Multitasking: Train yourself to listen and read at the same time, as you may need to do this during the test.
  • Check Spelling: Be careful with spelling, as errors can cost you marks. Pay attention to names, places, and any specific terms.
  • Don't Panic if You Miss Something: If you miss an answer, don't dwell on it. Focus on the next set of questions. You can still answer later questions correctly.
Remember to practice listening to a variety of English accents, as the IELTS test may include speakers with different accents. Regular practice will enhance your listening skills and overall performance.
